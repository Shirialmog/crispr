Base Editors and CrisPAM

Online DNA editing tools developed in Tel Aviv University

The bioinformatic tools we develop address the basic interests of molecular biologists in gene-editing experimental design. We currently offer 2 web tools for distinct purposes:
BE Matcher finds the most fit base editor to achieve the correction of a SNP. Both tools offer a database of human pathogenic SNPs (obtained from NCBI) and their corresponding Cas / Base editor to carry out the gene-editing aims.

CrisPam (Soon) identifies PAM sequences generated by a SNP. It may be helpful for targeting a pathogenic SNP in heterozygous models.
